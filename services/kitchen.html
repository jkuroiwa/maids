Kitchen Cleaning

The heart of your home — cleaned thoroughly, safely, and with aloha.

What's Included

Our kitchen cleaning goes well beyond a quick wipe-down. We deep-clean every surface, appliance, and fixture so your kitchen is truly sanitary — safe for cooking, safe for your family.

Countertops & Surfaces

  • Sanitize all countertop surfaces
  • Clean backsplash tiles and grout
  • Wipe down wall tiles near cooking areas

Appliances

  • Stove top: clean burners, grates, and drip pans
  • Oven exterior and control panel
  • Microwave inside and out
  • Refrigerator exterior (handles, door seals)
  • Dishwasher exterior
  • Small appliance exteriors

Sink & Fixtures

  • Scrub and sanitize sink basin
  • Polish faucet and handles
  • Clean garbage disposal rim

Cabinets & Storage

  • Wipe cabinet fronts and drawer faces
  • Clean cabinet handles and knobs
  • Dust tops of cabinets (reachable areas)

Floors

  • Sweep or vacuum floor
  • Mop with appropriate cleaner for floor type
  • Clean corners and baseboards

💡 Why a Clean Kitchen Matters

The kitchen is where bacteria and cross-contamination risks are highest. Regular professional cleaning reduces foodborne illness risk and keeps your family safe. Our eco-friendly products are food-safe and won't leave harmful residues.
