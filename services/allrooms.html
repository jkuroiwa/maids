🛋️ All-Room Cleaning

Your entire home — every room, top to bottom — immaculately cleaned.

What's Included

Our all-room cleaning covers your entire home — from the entryway to the master bedroom, living areas to hallways. We follow a consistent, detailed checklist to ensure nothing gets missed, every single visit.

All Rooms (General)

  • Dust all horizontal surfaces (shelves, tables, ledges)
  • Dust picture frames, decor, and knick-knacks
  • Wipe light switches and door knobs
  • Clean interior glass doors and mirrors
  • Empty and reline trash cans
  • Vacuum carpets, rugs, and upholstered furniture edges
  • Mop hard floors with appropriate cleaner
  • Wipe baseboards (accessible areas)

Living Room / Family Room

  • Dust ceiling fans and light fixtures
  • Clean TV screens and entertainment unit
  • Fluff and arrange throw pillows
  • Wipe down coffee tables and end tables

Bedrooms

  • Make beds and change linens (if provided)
  • Dust nightstands, dressers, and furniture tops
  • Vacuum under beds (if accessible)
  • Organize visible clutter (light tidying)

Entryway & Hallways

  • Sweep and mop entry floors
  • Wipe entry table and mirror
  • Dust hallway shelving and art

Lanai / Porch (on request)

  • Sweep lanai floor
  • Wipe outdoor furniture surfaces
  • Clean sliding door tracks

🏝️ Island Living Extras

We understand Oahu homes — trade winds bring in dust, open lanais invite geckos and insects, and salt air leaves residue on windows and surfaces. We're trained to handle the unique cleaning needs of island homes.

Frequency Options

  • Weekly — best for busy families
  • Bi-weekly — our most popular plan
  • Monthly — great for smaller homes
  • One-time / deep clean — any time
