Bathroom Cleaning

Sanitized, scrubbed, and sparkling — every bathroom in your home.

What's Included

Bathrooms are the most germ-prone rooms in any home. Our deep bathroom cleaning uses hospital-grade disinfectants (in eco-safe formulas) to eliminate bacteria, mold, and mildew — leaving your bathrooms genuinely clean.

Toilet

  • Disinfect inside bowl (under rim included)
  • Clean tank, seat, lid, and exterior base
  • Sanitize flush handle

Shower & Tub

  • Scrub shower walls and floor
  • Clean and descale shower door / curtain tracks
  • Scrub tub interior
  • Remove soap scum and hard water deposits
  • Clean showerhead

Sink & Vanity

  • Scrub and disinfect sink basin
  • Polish faucet and handles
  • Wipe vanity countertop and backsplash
  • Clean cabinet fronts under sink

Mirrors & Glass

  • Clean mirrors streak-free
  • Wipe glass shower enclosures (inside & out)

Floors & Finishing

  • Sweep and mop/disinfect tile floor
  • Scrub grout lines
  • Wipe baseboards
  • Empty trash and replace liner

💡 Hawaii's Humidity Challenge

Oahu's warm, humid climate creates ideal conditions for mold and mildew growth. Regular professional cleaning prevents buildup before it becomes a health hazard. Our products are effective against tropical mold while remaining safe for keiki and pets.
