Our Story

Maids of Honolulu was founded with a simple belief: every family on Oahu deserves a clean, healthy home — without the stress of doing it all themselves.

What started as a small team in Honolulu has grown into an island-wide service covering every corner of Oahu, from the windward communities of Kailua and Kaneohe to the leeward neighborhoods of Ewa Beach and Kapolei. Through it all, we've remained one team, one phone number, and one mission.

We are locally owned and operated, and we take pride in giving back to the community that supports us. Our cleaners are Oahu residents — neighbors serving neighbors.

We have received the Star Advertiser's Hawaii's Best of the Best Award, voted on by our community.
